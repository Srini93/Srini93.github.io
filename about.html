Process

How I work

I treat product design as a team sport — pairing curiosity with craft. Whether I'm sketching a flow, running a critique, or shipping pixel-perfect screens with engineering, I aim for clarity, momentum, and decisions everyone can stand behind.

01

Understand

Start with the problem, not the solution. I dig into user research, business context, and constraints, then frame what we're really solving — and why it matters now.

02

Explore & align

I sketch broadly before going deep — multiple directions, fast prototypes, and tight loops with PMs and engineers. Decisions are made together, with tradeoffs on the table.

03

Refine & ship

Polish in the details — micro-interactions, edge cases, accessibility, and copy. I stay close through dev to make sure what ships matches the intent. I'm comfortable with agentic coding and shipping work to production alongside engineering.

  • Default to clarity — short writing, named decisions, visible tradeoffs.
  • Prototype to think — interactive artifacts move conversations forward faster than slides.
  • Design with engineers — the best craft happens when design and code grow together.
  • Stay curious — I keep a running list of questions, books, and tools to learn from.
Giving back
