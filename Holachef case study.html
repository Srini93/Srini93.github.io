About the project

Holachef is a food-tech startup which curates chefs around and food cooked from them online. Everyday there is a change in menu and compostion of the cuisine. Being a product designer, I was responsible for designing multiple products that catered to the user and ones which also helped the process of the business easier.

3)Runners App - An app for delivery guys

1)To design an app for the delivery person to effecrively deliver orders and do pickups from the chefs and food centres.
2) Allow the delivery guys to stop and start their days trip at any point of time.
3) Plan their holidays from the application.
4) Get paid for the noof deliveries or pickup they had made for the day.
5) Calculate the distance and their number of trips a day.

Primary product applications

The people whom we involved in evaluating the solutions involved people both new spreadsheet users and power users who were regularly using spreadsheets in their day to day tasks. Each user was given the same task sheet with different variants of the user interface to carry out A/B testing and multivariate testing in order to determine the finest one in terms of usability and functionality.
